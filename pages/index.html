OUR VISION


This past year has no doubt been both a difficult and illuminating time for the LMU community. As we collectively prepare to make our return to the Bluff, rebuilding and reforming the student-body is of the utmost importance. We look forward to welcoming students back to campus, those who are new and those who are returning. To emphasize, first and foremost, our priority is the LMU student body. We commit ourselves to uplifting student voices and experiences, intentionally fostering a socially just and inclusive environment, and delivering an overall dynamic year as we work to transition from a virtual platform to an in-person experience. Join us to Build Back the Bluff.

OUR 3 PILLARS




Brion and Camille are committed to prioritizing student wellness as our community navigates a historic return back to in-person campus life.



Brion and Camille understand that what makes LMU so great is its driven and energetic students. As we return to in-person learning, we dedicate ourselves to rebuild the passionate Lion community that we have come to know and love.



Brion and Camille ensure the student body that the important work of past ASLMU administrations to reform our university will not be forgotten in our plans for the future.
