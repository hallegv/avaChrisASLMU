OUR VISION


We believe in a student government centered on student support. Our priority is to advocate for the well-being of all students; commit more resources to student organizations, student artists, and student activists; and hold the university accountable in its commitments to create a more just and anti-racist environment. A community built on student empowerment, support, and advocacy is a community we will achieve through our collective voices and unified spirit.

OUR THREE PILLARS


Support

We are committed to enhancing and strengthening support for all students, increasing access to club and organization funding, pushing the university to allocate more funding to student mental health resources, and recognizing ASLMU’s place as a resource hub for students.

Campus Life Improvements

We know that positive campus experiences are essential to living and studying at LMU. We are determined to make sure all Lions have access to high quality and safe eating options, more places to park on campus, and more transportation off campus via our Lyft program. We also believe International Lions should have sufficient access to enough T-work jobs and will dedicate our administration to finding and creating solutions to these problems.

Empowerment

We believe in empowering and enabling all members of the LMU community to create the world we want to live in. Leveraging the full financial power of ASLMU, we plan to institute an advocacy fund that will financially support the work of both individual students and student organizations while also continuing ASLMU’s commitment to create a fly-in program for prospective First-Generation and Low Income students. We also plan to take measures to hold the university accountable in their commitments to creating a just and anti-racist campus.
